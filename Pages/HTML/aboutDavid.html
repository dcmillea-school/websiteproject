About David

Writer • Storyteller • Leader • Believer • Fighter

Who I Am

I love writing. Right now, I’m wrapping up a book I’ve been working on, and I hope it’s just the beginning. I want to write many more and, God-willing, become a well-renowned author whose words move people. One day, I hope one of my stories is turned into a movie. That would be a dream come true.

I’m passionate about making the world a better place — not just in big gestures, but in the daily effort of doing better than I did yesterday. I strive to make choices that honor my family, my countries, and the future generations that will come after me.

I consider myself a leader. I'm always learning how to become a better one — through faith, responsibility, and growth. I believe in God — in Jesus Christ — and I fully trust His plan for my life.
